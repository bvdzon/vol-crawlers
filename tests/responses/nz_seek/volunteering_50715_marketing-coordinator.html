Marketing Coordinator

for West Auckland Academic Mentoring more by this organisation (2)

Education Long Term Auckland

About this opportunity

West Auckland Academic Mentoring provides free academic support provided by volunteers for West Auckland secondary school students.

2018 will be our second year of operation and we are looking to reach a larger number of students and mentors. You will be responsible for spreading the word about WAAM to help us reach more potential mentors and students. This will include:
- Creating content and regularly posting on our Facebook page, and developing other social media channels as appropriate
- Building relationships with schools and getting their help with reaching students.
- Producing press releases and contacting local media
- Exploring multiple avenues for volunteer recruitment

Ideally you will be a marketing student or have previous experience in marketing or volunteer recruitment. Knowledge of and contacts in the education sector would also be valuable, as would proven social media skills. Ideally, you would also be interested in being a mentor, as this is the best way to get to know what we do.

If you have any combination of the experience above and want to use it to fight educational inequality, please apply using our online application form at https://goo.gl/forms/aPHVJp09md2fCw8O2.


Additional requirements

A police check will be required if you also want to volunteer as a mentor.

* May be arranged via the Organisation. Details to be provided on expression of interest.
Time Required

Approximately 3-5 hours per week. Ideally you will also be able to attend mentoring sessions on Wednesday evenings, 6:30pm-8:30pm, during the school term.
